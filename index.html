When Local Clinics Close, Women Travel Farther

Comparing each state’s Access Gap (% of women living in a county without a clinic) and the % traveling out of state for care (2020).

Write-up

This visualization explores how clinic accessibility impacts travel distances across U.S. states. It highlights which regions show the largest “access gaps” and how these relate to the percentage of patients traveling for care.
